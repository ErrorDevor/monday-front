About the project

The Monday service is a new approach to buying and selling services online. Now, if you want to order a service or monetize your skills, it's easier, safer and faster than before.

What opportunities does the Monday website provide?

We have set ourselves the task of creating an online platform where customers and service providers can find each other without risking anything or spending too much time.

Option one: you are the customer. You don’t need to look for specific exchanges and dubious groups on social networks. You open a catalog where you can see information about the services themselves and about those who offer them. Option two: you are a performer. You don't need to spend a lot of time and money on your portfolio and promoting your pages. You add your services to the catalog - customers come to you themselves. In both cases, the administration of the Monday website acts as a guarantor. We make sure that the information is reliable, services are completed on time, and remuneration for good work is paid without delays or fraud.

Guarantees of the Monday site for customers and performers

Using our service, you don't have to worry about anything:

The interface is optimized to make it easy for you to communicate with your counterparty. Services are categorized and organized. You can filter them by cost and other parameters. Money is guaranteed to be paid if the work is completed, and is also guaranteed to be returned to the account if not. The default price for the service is 500 rubles. Performers can set their own price based on the principles of fair competition. In addition to the main service, you can add additional options.

We work in all areas of modern freelancing

The Monday website is a universal platform where you can order services in the following areas:

  1. graphics and design - images of any complexity and purpose;
  2. creation of websites, applications, modification of program code;
  3. SEO optimization, attracting traffic to the website;
  4. contextual advertising, mailings, marketing.
  5. and other categories of services.

And these are just the main, most popular areas. You can find a complete list of categories and available services by looking at the site menu. Just a few mouse clicks separate you from the most modern, affordable and yet easy-to-use service for ordering and selling online services.
